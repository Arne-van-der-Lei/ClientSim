Remarks

Currently only used for DesktopTracking, but can be extended for VR and even fake VR implementations. Sends Events:

  • ClientSimOnPlayerHeightUpdateEvent
  • ClientSimOnTrackingScaleUpdateEvent Listens to Events:
  • ClientSimOnPlayerHeightUpdateEvent
